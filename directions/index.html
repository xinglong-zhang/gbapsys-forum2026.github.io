Venue

Forum 2026 will be held at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Hong Kong, China.

Getting to CUHK

By MTR (Mass Transit Railway): Take the East Rail Line to University Station. CUHK campus is directly accessible from the station.

By Taxi: Taxis are widely available throughout Hong Kong. Ask the driver for “The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Sha Tin.”

From Hong Kong International Airport: Take the Airport Express to Kowloon or Hong Kong Station, then transfer to the East Rail Line heading towards Lo Wu/Lok Ma Chau and alight at University Station.
